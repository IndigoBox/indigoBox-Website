The Speed of Time

indigoBox's awesome time-bending game


Time-Bending Action

Combined with stealth, speed, and strength skills, use the concepts of time dilation to creatively move through the story, engaging in a wide variety of combat tactics.

Set in Chicago

An energetic plot meets a historic, magnificent, and energetic city. Become part of magnificent Chicago as you pass every streetlight, every terminal, and every mile after magnificent mile. All while interacting with a vivid and complex plot.

Made With Unity

One of the most advanced game engines is being put to high use with elaborate physics, lifelike materials, and much more. Furthermore, Unity also allows for simple optimization, creating a smoother gameplay experience.
