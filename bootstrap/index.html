Currently,

Student Council uses a minimalist Bootstrap template for their website, but with my vision to enhance it with other features Bootstrap has to offer, it could be more in touch with the student body.

Alerts

These can be used to show quick, temporary messages to anyone who visits the website.

Dropdowns

The Student Council website could benefit from a bit of reorganizing, such as telling people where the constitution is, instead of just assuming they'll find it in the About section. Dropdowns will allow students to select where they want to go.

Carousels

These can be used to show off a variety of pictures, slideshows(for things like election instructions), or other information.
