Background

indigoBox Studios is an innovative Chicago-based software development collective focused on a broad array of projects conceived by its individual members. Founded in 2012 as an extension of personal software endeavors turned collaborative, indigoBox now serves as a focused development studio dedicated to bringing cutting-edge software projects to a variety of markets.

Featured Projects

  • Space Demo: A Unity-based exploration of our solar system

  • Carpe: Intuitive, social scheduling for individuals and organizations

  • Scroll Stop: A Chrome extension to keep you on task

  • Sprout: A laid-back platformer for relaxing play

  • The Speed of Time: An innovative, time-warping FPS game
